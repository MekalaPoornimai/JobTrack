Frequently Asked Questions

5 questions
🔍 How do I view a student's full profile from the Pipeline? +

To view a student's complete profile from the Student Pipeline:

  1. Navigate to My Students → Student Pipeline from the sidebar
  2. Find the student in the table
  3. Click the "View" button in the Actions column

The Student Profile View shows their full application history, skills, upcoming interviews, your advising notes, and key stats. You can also add a note directly from there.

📝 How do I add an advising note for a student? +

You can add advising notes in two ways:

  • From Student Pipeline: Click the "Add Note" button next to the student's row
  • From Student Profile View: Click the "+ Add Note for Student" button in the top-right or inside the Advisor Notes card
  • From Advising Notes page: Use the quick form on the right sidebar

When adding a note, you can set the category, priority level, add tags, link to a specific application, and set a follow-up reminder date.

⚠️ How do I intervene when a student is flagged as "At Risk"? +

When a student shows the ⚠️ At Risk or ⚠️ Struggling status:

  1. Click the "Intervene" button (appears instead of "View" for at-risk students)
  2. You'll go directly to the Add Note form pre-populated with the student's info
  3. Select "⚠️ Intervention" as the note category
  4. Set priority to High
  5. Document your action plan and set a follow-up reminder

Students are also highlighted on your Dashboard under "⚠️ Needs Attention" for quick access.

🔒 Can students see my advising notes? +

By default, advising notes are visible to students. You have two options to control visibility:

  • Per-note: Toggle the 🔒 "Private Note" switch when creating a note — that note will only be visible to advisors and admins
  • Default setting: Go to Settings → Privacy → enable "Private Note Default" to make all new notes private by default

Intervention-type notes are automatically marked as advisor-only for student wellbeing and privacy.

📊 How do I filter the Student Pipeline by status? +

At the top of the Student Pipeline page, you'll find filter tabs:

  • All Students (24) — shows every assigned student
  • Actively Applying (18) — students with recent applications
  • In Interviews (8) — students with scheduled interviews
  • Offers (9) — students who received offers
  • ⚠️ Needs Help (3) — flagged at-risk students

Click any tab to filter the table. You can also use the search bar to find a specific student by name.

⚙️ How do I update my notification preferences? +

To manage your notifications:

  1. Click Settings in the left sidebar (⚙️ icon)
  2. Scroll to the "🔔 Notification Preferences" section
  3. Toggle each notification type on or off
  4. Choose your preferred delivery method: Email, In-App, or SMS
  5. Click "Save Preferences"

You can receive alerts for student activity (applications, interviews, offers), inactivity warnings, follow-up reminders, and weekly pipeline summaries.
